Purpose of the Website

The goal of this website is to create a Glow Up Guide that helps teens improve their confidence, habits, and self-care through simple and realistic tips. The website focuses on mental health, physical wellness, style, and routines in a positive and motivating way.

Who This Website Is For

This website is designed for teens who want to become more confident, organized, and healthy. It is made for anyone looking for encouraging, easy-to-follow advice instead of unrealistic standards.

What the Website Includes

  • Motivation and confidence tips
  • Self-care and hygiene ideas
  • Healthy routines and habits
  • Style and personal expression advice
  • Wellness topics like sleep, balance, and productivity

Future Pages

As this project continues, more pages will be added, including Mental Glow Up, Physical Glow Up, Style Glow Up, Health and Wellness, Daily Routines, a Glow Up Checklist, FAQ, and a Contact and Feedback form.
